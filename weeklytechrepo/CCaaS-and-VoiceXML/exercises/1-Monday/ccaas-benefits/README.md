# Lab: Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis

## The Scenario

You are a Solutions Architect working for a mid-sized retail company. The company currently operates a massive Legacy On-Premise Call Center. There are physical PBX switchboards in the basement, hundreds of desk phones that break constantly, and a dedicated IT staff just to manage the telecom wires.

The CIO has asked you to write a brief, formal proposal analyzing the benefits of migrating this entire operation to heavily-virtualized CCaaS platform (like Amazon Connect or Genesys Cloud).

## Deliverables

1. Navigate to the `starter_template` directory and open `TCO_Analysis.md`.
2. Fill out the four required sections:
    * **Capital Expenditures (CapEx) vs. Operating Expenditures (OpEx):** Explain how CCaaS shifts the financial model from buying expensive hardware upfront to a pay-as-you-go subscription.
    * **Scalability & Elasticity:** How does the company handle Black Friday spikes under the old system vs the new CCaaS system?
    * **Work-From-Anywhere Capability:** Contrast the physical limitations of desk phones with the browser-based WebRTC softphones of CCaaS.
    * **Maintenance & Upgrades:** Who handles security patches and new feature rollouts in both scenarios?

## Definition of Done

- The Markdown document is completely filled out.
* The arguments presented clearly demonstrate an understanding of Cloud Computing benefits specifically applied to the Telecommunications sector.
